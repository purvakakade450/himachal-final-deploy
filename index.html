        About HP Monsoon Portal

        Research initiative by Centre for Climate Studies, IIT Bombay — real-time rainfall forecasts & community flood reporting for Himachal Pradesh villages.

        🎯 Objectives

        • Village-level real-time forecasts via Open-Meteo
        • Community flood & landslide reporting on satellite map
        • Early warning for mountain communities during monsoon
        • Climate resilience research for the Himalayan region

        🛰️ Data Sources

        • Weather: Open-Meteo — real-time, free
        • Satellite: Esri World Imagery
        • Terrain: Esri World Shaded Relief
        • Maps: OpenStreetMap contributors

        ⚠️ Forecasts from numerical models — always follow official IMD advisories.
